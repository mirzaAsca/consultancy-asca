## Everything In This Paper Runs Automatically For Every FlyRank Client.

You don't do any of this yourself. We do it for you. Here's the full list.

---

### We Create Your Content

- We write full SEO articles for you. Not outlines. Not drafts. Done articles.
- We write them in any language you need.
- We write as many as you need. Every day if that's the plan.
- We generate the images.
- We write the meta titles and descriptions.
- We optimize every piece for Google and for AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity).
- We localize your content so it reads natural in every market you sell in.

### We Publish It

- We push your content live to your site. Shopify, WordPress, Webflow, Ghost — whatever you use.
- We do it automatically. No uploads. No formatting. No back and forth.
- We submit every new page to Google for indexing the same day.
- We track whether Google actually picked it up.

### We Watch Every Page, Every Day

- We pull your Search Console data, your Analytics data, and your AI referral data into one place.
- We score every page with a health check that looks at impressions, ranking position, click-through rate, and scroll depth — all at once.
- We track which pages are growing, which are flat, and which are falling.
- We track AI traffic from ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Copilot, Claude, and Meta. By provider. By day. By content type.
- We track how your content ages, when it peaks, and when it starts losing ground.
- We track which search queries your pages actually rank for — and whether those queries match what the page was built to capture.

### We Tell You Exactly What To Fix (And In What Order)

- We maintain a live optimization queue. Not a report you read once. A running list that updates itself.
- We flag pages that need a refresh and rank them by how much they can recover.
- We flag pages that get seen but don't get clicked, and size the impressions sitting on the table.
- We flag pages almost on page one and show what a small push could unlock.
- We flag quick wins — stuff you can act on this week.
- We find where your own pages fight each other for the same keyword, and we measure how many impressions and clicks that costs you.

### We Fix It

- Our AI agents rewrite weak titles and descriptions.
- They improve readability and formatting.
- They update stale sections in old content.
- They push the changes live.
- They run inside the same workflow as everything else. No separate tool.

### We Keep The Loop Running

- This isn't a one-time audit. It runs every week.
- New content gets created and published on schedule.
- Old content gets flagged when it starts to slip.
- Fixes get queued, made, and tracked.
- You see what happened. We see what to do next.
